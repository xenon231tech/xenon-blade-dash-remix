# Game Glossary

## Terms
- **Combo**: Consecutive enemy kills without taking damage
- **Dash**: Quick movement ability with cooldown
- **HP**: Health Points - player health
- **Upgrade**: Permanent stat increase chosen between waves
- **Wave**: Round of enemies with increasing difficulty
- **Power-up**: Temporary boost (Speed, Shield, Double Score)

## Enemy Types
- **Normal**: Standard red demon
- **Fast**: Quick fire spirit
- **Tank**: Slow but tanky golem
- **Swarm**: Small imp enemies
- **Shooter**: Ice mage with projectiles
- **Teleport**: Phantom that teleports
- **Splitter**: Slime that splits
- **Boss**: Demon Lord with special attacks
