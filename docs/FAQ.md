# Frequently Asked Questions

## Gameplay

**Q: How do I get high scores?**
A: Build combos by killing enemies without taking damage. Use upgrades strategically.

**Q: What's the best strategy?**
A: Focus on survivability first, then damage. Kite enemies and use dash wisely.

**Q: How many waves are there?**
A: Waves scale infinitely, but difficulty caps at wave 20+.

## Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Yes, the game works offline after initial load.

**Q: Is there a mobile version?**
A: Yes, touch controls are fully supported.

## Account

**Q: Do I need an account?**
A: No, game saves locally to browser.

**Q: Can I sync saves?**
A: Not yet, but planned for future versions.

**Q: How do I delete my save?**
A: Clear browser local storage or use the reset button.
