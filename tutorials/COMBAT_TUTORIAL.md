# Combat Tutorial

## Attack Types

### Slash Attack
- Click to attack
- 100px range
- 0.3s cooldown
- 180° arc

### Dash Attack
- Space to dash
- 150px distance
- 1s cooldown
- Invulnerability frames

### Combo Attack
- Kill without damage
- Multiplier increases
- Bonus points awarded
- Reset on damage

## Enemy Patterns

### Normal Enemy
- Direct approach
- Predictable movement
- Easy to kite
- Good for learning

### Fast Enemy
- Rapid movement
- Quick attacks
- Requires spacing
- Good for practice

### Tank Enemy
- Slow movement
- High health
- Requires patience
- Good for damage

## Combat Techniques

### Kiting
- Attack while moving
- Maintain distance
- Use dash to escape
- Master timing

### Positioning
- Stay away from edges
- Keep enemies spread
- Use terrain wisely
- Plan escape routes

### Timing
- Learn attack patterns
- Predict movements
- Attack between hits
- Dodge incoming attacks
