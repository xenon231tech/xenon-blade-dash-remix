# Upgrade System Tutorial

## Upgrade Types

### Vitality
- Increases max health
- Best for beginners
- Survivability focused
- Stacks well

### Swift Feet
- Increases movement speed
- Mobility improvement
- Evasion focused
- Synergizes with dash

### Sharp Blade
- Increases slash damage
- Offense focused
- Kills faster
- Scales well

### Wide Arc
- Increases attack range
- Area improvement
- Better coverage
- Synergizes with damage

### Treasure Hunter
- Increases score multiplier
- Economy focused
- Long-term value
- Compounds

### Quick Dash
- Reduces dash cooldown
- Mobility improvement
- Evasion focused
- Powerful synergy

### Barrier
- Temporary shield
- Defense focused
- Situational
- Great for emergencies

### Rejuvenate
- Heals 1 HP
- Recovery focused
- Situational
- Good for low health

## Upgrade Strategy

### Early Game
- Pick Vitality
- Build health pool
- Learn patterns
- Don't specialize

### Mid Game
- Choose upgrade path
- Synergize choices
- Balance offense/defense
- Plan ahead

### Late Game
- Optimize synergies
- Perfect execution
- Maximize score
- Compete
